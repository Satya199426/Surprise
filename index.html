Happy Birthday!
    

Follow These Steps to Create Your Surprise:

  1. Upload an Image
    Click on the "Upload Image" button.
    Select a photo from your device to upload.
  2. Customize the Greeting Text (Optional)
    Enter your custom message in the "Enter your custom greeting" text box.
    Click on the "Update Text" button to display your message on the card.
  3. Reveal the Surprise
    Click on the "Reveal Card" button.
    Watch the cover slide away and enjoy the firecracker blast effect.
  4. Download Your Greeting
    Click on the "Download Greeting" button.
    Your personalized greeting card will be downloaded to your device.
  5. Clear All Inputs
    To reset and start over, click on the "Clear All" button.
